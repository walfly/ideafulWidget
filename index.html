Ideaful widget

Ideaful.co offers a widget that allows you to embed a stream of ideas from any challenge on your page. Simply append the following script tage to your page.

<script src="http://ideaful.co/embed/challenge/[CHALLENGE_ID].js?container=[ID_OF_ELEMENT_TO_APPEND_TO]&w=[DESIRED_WIDTH]" type="text/javascript"></script>
